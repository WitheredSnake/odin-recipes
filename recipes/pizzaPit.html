Pizza Pit Pizza

A delicious Margherita Pizza resembling some Fortnite Pizza Pit Pizza.

Recipe from loveandlemons.com

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Heaping 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Spread pizza sauce onto the dough
  3. Top with fresh mozzarella and tomatoes
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned.
  5. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
