Slurp Juice

A delicious non-alcoholic, refreshing and colorful ombre sour melon flavored drink inspired by Fortnite

Recipe from sincitybartender.com

Ingredients

  • 5 oz melon syrup
  • 5 oz apple syrup
  • 4 oz Limeade
  • 3 oz Ocean Lemonade
  • As much ice as you prefer

Steps

  1. Shake everything but the lemonade with ice
  2. Pour into glass with plenty of ice
  3. Carefully layer the lemonade on top
  4. Enjoy!
